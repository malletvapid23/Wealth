CMRP Rate: The Fountain

CMRP Rate determines the size of the pool, if it gets bigger, the amount that we can control through the pool gets bigger as well. In the previous samples, in order to make the difference apparent, this number was fixed to 10% of the total spendings in the system.

At this stage, since the application will be used a simulation tool, we can use and test any number.

In practice, how these resource pools and their amounts will be realized may vary and probably be determined by the idea that how much they can make a difference. In any case, even if the amount to be distributed would be very small, affecting the market, publicly rewarding the organizations that are beneficial for the society, could have an important positive effect.
