Sector Index: The Big Picture

One of the issues in the current system is that the consumers usually only interact with the organizations about their daily needs.

For example, one might think that education sector is more important for the society than most of the other sectors. However, to be able to interact and make transactions with this sector, one should either be a student or a relative of a student. Otherwise, it would be out of the routine and cannot be supported in a regular basis. Instead, one could spend its income on clothing, cosmetics, entertainment and so on, that might have a less priority.

Sector Index could gives us the opportunity to see the overall of the system and let us distribute the resources to the sectors by comparing them.

In the next part, you will see six important sectors. Every organization under these sectors is trying to solve one of the issues that the society has. One is developing video games to entertain, another is trying to educate, one is trying to cure cancer, another is trying to make you look good. We believe, when we could directly choose which sectors have more priority and could affect how to distribute the resources, the result would be very different than the current one.
