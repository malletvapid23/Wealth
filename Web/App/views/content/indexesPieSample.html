Indexes Pie

In the previous samples there was one index per pool. However in most cases, there will be multiple indexes within a CMRP. Since not every index is equally important, their share within the pool needs to be determined as well.

Indexes view allows us to compare and rate indexes within each other.

  • By using + button increase Number of Sales, initially all organizations will have same Total Income.
  • Click on Index Income link and switch to Indexes view to see the details of each index.
  • Rate each index based on their importance by using + and - buttons at the bottom of them.
  • Click to Index Income link again to switch back to the previous view.
  • Switch Display to All Ratings to see the average of all users' ratings.
